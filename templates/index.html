The Masked Manual is a free & open source app that puts surgical mask and respirator data in your pocket.

Download from the app store

Available for iOS only.

Features

Information on surgical masks and respirators.

2000+ masks

Including NIOSH approved N95 respirators, FDA approved surgical masks, and masks authorized by the FDA for emergency usage during COVID-19.

Scan to search quickly

Image recognition helps quickly locate masks from their packaging.

Make the choice to stay safe

Easily compare mask qualifications and safety information.

Open source

MIT licensed, contributions welcome.
